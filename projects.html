Our Projects

At Fluxonix, we have successfully completed various projects that demonstrate our expertise and dedication. Below is a selection of our work, showcasing our capabilities across multiple domains.

Advanced Networking Solutions

We have provided comprehensive networking solutions, including tunneling/VPN, firewalling, and general networking services. Our projects have ranged from small-scale configurations to complex subnetting and routing solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Satellite Broadband Engineering

Our team has extensive experience in satellite broadband solutions, including VSAT services with providers like Optus, Pivotel, and Eutelsat. We have engineered and deployed BGAN IoT solutions in collaboration with Inmarsat and Pivotel, and have worked with the latest Iridium NEXT constellation for advanced global communications.

We also stay at the forefront of modern satellite technologies, working with OneWeb and Starlink to provide cutting-edge broadband solutions. With strong industry contacts, we can provision and access the best business satellite products available.

Custom Software Development

Our software development expertise includes creating tailored solutions to meet specific business needs. Whether it's enterprise-level software or custom applications for specialized hardware, our team delivers robust and scalable solutions.

Innovative Electronics Engineering

From designing complex circuits to developing embedded systems, we excel in all aspects of electronics engineering. Our projects have included designing custom PCB layouts, prototyping new hardware, and ensuring compliance with EMC/EMI regulations.

Comprehensive Security Solutions

Security is at the core of our services. We have implemented robust security solutions, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and secure networking protocols, to protect our clients' critical infrastructure from cyber threats.

Cloud Engineering and Integration

We have successfully migrated businesses to cloud environments, leveraging platforms like Amazon AWS. Our cloud solutions include infrastructure setup, application deployment, and ongoing management, enabling businesses to harness the power of the cloud efficiently.

Embedded Linux and Board Development

Our experience in embedded Linux and board development includes creating custom hardware and software solutions for specialized applications. We have worked on system-level designs and optimized performance for various embedded systems.

Industry-Specific Expertise

Our team members hold qualifications in networking, electronics engineering, and software development. With our diverse skill set, we have delivered projects across various industries, including telecommunications, IoT, and cloud services.

Get in touch

If you would like to discuss a project or learn more about our services, please contact us. We're here to help you achieve your goals through innovative technology solutions.
