Our Projects

At Fluxonix, we have successfully completed various projects that demonstrate our expertise and dedication. Here are some highlights of our work:

Network Engineering Consulting

We provided expert consulting services in network engineering, helping businesses design and implement robust and scalable network infrastructures that ensure reliable connectivity and optimal performance.

Strong Motion Seismic Recorders

We developed advanced strong motion seismic recorders that accurately measure and record seismic activity, crucial for monitoring and analyzing earthquake data to enhance safety and preparedness.

Camera Streaming Solutions

We created innovative camera streaming solutions that provide high-quality, real-time video feeds for security surveillance, live event broadcasting, and remote monitoring.

IoT Sensors with LoRaWAN, NB-IoT, and Cat-M1

We designed and developed IoT sensors using LoRaWAN, NB-IoT, and Cat-M1 technologies, deployed in diverse environments for applications like environmental monitoring and smart agriculture.

Provisioning Private Cellular APNs

Our expertise includes provisioning private cellular Access Point Names (APNs) to enhance network security and management, helping businesses set up secure and reliable private networks for their IoT devices and applications.

Remote Monitoring Solutions for Field Deployments

We developed comprehensive remote monitoring solutions for field deployments of IT hardware, enabling real-time monitoring of equipment status and performance to ensure timely maintenance and minimize downtime.

IoT Sensor Applications Using MQTT

We developed IoT sensor applications utilizing the MQTT protocol for efficient and reliable data transmission, supporting use cases like smart home systems, industrial automation, and environmental monitoring.

Cloud Solutions with Amazon AWS

We leveraged Amazon AWS to create scalable and secure cloud solutions, including infrastructure setup, application deployment, and management, ensuring clients can efficiently harness the power of the cloud.
